Title: EULA 2025-03-18: Summary of changes

----

Intro:

Licenses for Kirby 4+ are available in Basic and Enterprise variants. Which license variant to choose depends on the yearly revenue of the individual or company that the project is created for. We have clarified our EULA to make this unambiguous.

As the change does not constrain your rights, the updated terms become binding by publication on the Kirby website for all Kirby 4+ licenses.

In particular, we have changed the following:

----

Text:

## Clarifications

- We rewrote the definition "Client" to include indirect clients (e.g. when working as a freelancer for an agency that works on a client website).
