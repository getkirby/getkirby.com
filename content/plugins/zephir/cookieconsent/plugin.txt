Integrates the [orestbida/cookieconsent](https://github.com/orestbida/cookieconsent) JS plugin into Kirby 3.
Comes with two pre-configured types (simple / customizable) to get you started quickly, and can be extended with all the original options.
Supports multilingual websites.
