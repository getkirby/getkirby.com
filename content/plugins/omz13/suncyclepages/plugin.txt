----

Text: 

----

Repository: https://github.com/omz13/kirby3-suncyclepages

----

Category: dates

----

Description: Embargo pages until sunrise date and withdraw at sunset date

----

Example:

```php
$articles = page('blog')
    ->children()
    ->listed()
    ->isUnderEmbargo(false)
    ->isSunset(false);
```
