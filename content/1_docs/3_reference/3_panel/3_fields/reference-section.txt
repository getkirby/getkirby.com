Title: Fields

----

Excerpt:

The (glossary: Panel) offers a wide range of field types that you can use to structure your content in the best possible way. From a simple text field over multiselect fields for users, files or pages to the structure field for multiple entries like addresses or events, you can already do a whole lot. And if that is not enough, you can create (link: docs/reference/plugins/extensions/fields text: any custom field) you need. The possibilities are endless.
