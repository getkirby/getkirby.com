Title: Sections

----

Excerpt:

Sections help you to organize content in the (glossary: Panel) or provide additional information or functionality. Kirby comes with 4 default section types.

----

Text:

Sections are be defined in your (glossary: blueprint text: blueprints) and help you to structure your content in the best possible way. Sections can be defined once and reused and extended in different blueprints.

In addition to the default section types, you can also **(link: docs/reference/plugins/extensions/sections text: create your own custom sections)**.
